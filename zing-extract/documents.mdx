title: Documents

Zing Extract primarily processes two type of documents:

- Financial Statements - E.g. Bank statements, payslips etc.
- Identification Documents - E.g Driver's License, NIN Slip, Passport etc.

<Note>Have any documents you think we should be processing? [Tell us about it](mailto:hello@zingfi.co)</Note>
