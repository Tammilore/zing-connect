title: Pricing
---
We charge a flat fee between 75 - 100 Naira per page extracted. The cost is discounted as your volume of pages increase. 

| Page Range | Fee per page |
| --- | --- |
| 1 - 1000 | 100 NGN |
| 1001 - 10000 | 80 NGN |
| 10001+ | 75 NGN |

<Info>This means your first 1000 pages would cost 100 Naira each, then your next 80 Naira and the costs keep going down as your volume increase.</Info>

We’re still modifying our pricing accordingly so feel free to shoot us an email if you have any feedback that could help us improve. We’re also giving out your first 500 pages for free so sign up [here](https://tally.so/r/mKpkWV) to get access or send an email to [hello@zingfi.co](mailto:hello@zingfi.co).
